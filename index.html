Forget/wisdom

Forgetting the past is a blessing and a curse. It blocks wisdom and creates a seemingly ever lasting challenge. Although it helps with moving on, it almost blocks out future referencing. Future reference in the case of learning from your past, if you can't remember, how can you have learnt.
Possible ways to overcome:

  1. Reflect on the day that has passed
  2. Note the lessons and/ scenarios that have occurred prior to the time of reflection
  3. Understand.
The journey to attain wisdom is ever lasting, things are ever changing. I see wisdom as being power as a pose to knowledge, BC wisdom is learning from your mistakes and understanding how to not repeat where as knowledge is knowing in, a sense. Wisdom allows you to act on experience and allows you to be able to predict what could happen, I say "could" BC like stated previously things are constantly changing. I assume things are constantly changing BC if things were to not change everything would just be predictable, pointless.
